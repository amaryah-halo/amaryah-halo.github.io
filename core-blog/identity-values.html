Identity, Values, and Strengths Exploration

Friday, 3 September 2021

Explain a situation where you have had an ethical decision to make. Discuss how you weighed up the values involved in that decision, the decision you made and the consequences of the action you took.

I used to be a housekeeper for a hotel and the working environment wasn't always best. We worked in pairs and covered a floor of the hotel each. I was paired with my friend who was having a hard day because of something that had recently happened in her life. Because of this she made a mistake that I should've reported her for. However, I weighed out everything that had been happening that day and made the decision to fix the problem, keep it between ourselves, and have the conversation on what she should do next time instead of doing what she did. I'm happy with the action I made - I put people first before my job. However, if it did become a repeated occurence (which it didn't) I would've had to make the decision to report her to my manager.

Describe how your culture has influenced your values and identity.

In my culture family, community, and looking out for eachother are central in all we do. For example, whenever we go to Niue our family will have cooked us an umu of taro, fish, and chicken which they'll bring over for us to eat as soon as we land. They'll also drop of baskets of coconuts and uga's throughout our whole trip. Having that strong family and community culture has influenced me to always value others over myself while stilling valuing what I can bring to the table. My culture has humbled me to also learn that everyone no matter what background or status has something to bring to the table. My identity is my family and they're a strong pillar of for my values and who I am :)

I'll take breaks, journal, and re-evaluate what needs to be done and how I'm going to do it. That may include going for a walk, catching up with a friend, or watching a movie to clear my head and reset it. That way I can come back to my studies refocused!
